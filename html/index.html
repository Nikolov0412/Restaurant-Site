Our story

Located in the heart of Sofia, Hilltop Gurme is an award-winning restaurant driven by a passion for gourmet, an ingredient for the heart of Gourmet kitchem. Chef Fermin and his team curate a seasonally driven menu with an emphasis on bringing together traditional Gourmet cooking techniques with local ingredients to create a unique and exciting dining experience. Our bar offers some of Sofia's most unique cocktails and succulent spirits state-side, as well as highlighting a rotating selection of natural wines.
